Realme Narzo New Review

The Realme Narzo 50A is a budget-focused smartphone (released Oct 2021) featuring a 6.5-inch HD+ display, MediaTek Helio G85 processor, and a massive 6000mAh battery with 18W charging. It boasts a 50MP AI triple camera setup, 4GB RAM, and 64GB/128GB storage options, targeting performance and longevity
Key Specifications & Features:
Performance: MediaTek Helio G85 octa-core processor (up to 2.0GHz).
Battery: 6000mAh with 18W quick charge; supports reverse charging.
Camera: 50MP main sensor + 2MP macro + 2MP portrait lens; 8MP front camera.
Display: 16.5 cm (6.5-inch) HD+ mini-drop display with 570 nits peak brightness.
Storage & RAM: 4GB LPDDR4X RAM with 64GB or 128GB internal storage (expandable up to 256GB via microSD).
Design: Diagonal stripe design with a rear-mounted fingerprint sensor.
OS: Realme UI 2.0 based on Android 11.
