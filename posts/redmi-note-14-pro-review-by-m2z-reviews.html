Redmi Note 14 Pro Review By M2Z Reviews

The Redmi Note 14 5G features a 6.67-inch 120Hz AMOLED display with up to 2100 nits peak brightness, powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7025-Ultra chipset. Key specs include a 50MP Sony LYT-600 main camera with OIS, a 5110mAh battery with 45W fast charging, IP64 rating, and Android 14 with HyperOS.
Key Specifications & Features Performance: MediaTek Dimensity 7025-Ultra (6nm) octa-core processor with up to 8GB RAM and 256GB storage. Display: 6.67-inch FHD+ AMOLED, 120Hz refresh rate, 240Hz touch sampling rate, 2100 nits peak brightness, Corning Gorilla Glass 5. Camera: Rear: 50MP Sony LYT-600 main camera (f/1.5, OIS) + 2MP macro. Front: 20MP selfie camera. Battery & Charging: 5110mAh battery, 45W fast charging. Software: Android 14 with HyperOS 1.0 (promises 2 years of OS updates and 4 years of security updates). Durability & Design: IP64 dust and water resistance, plastic frame and back. Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.3, 3.5mm headphone jack, Dual SIM. Audio: Dual stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos.
